class Employee

attributes:
1. position (array)
2. Age (integer)
3. salary(float)
4. hours (integer)

Methods

 1. add_hours (the hour count is equal to how many hours the person has. Employer can
add more hours to the person by increments of 1)

 2. give_raise( from the original salary a 10% increase will be added to the salary)

 3. change_age (change current age by increment of 1)

 4. add_position (add a new position to the current array.)
