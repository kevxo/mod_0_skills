class Employee

1. Name (string)
2. Age (integer)
3. salary(float)
4. hours (integer)

Methods

old_enough_to_work(collects the name and checks if the age is above 18 to be eligible )
works_part_time (checks to see if hours worked is under 25.)
total_pay (collects the amount hours and multiplies the amount of hours worked)
