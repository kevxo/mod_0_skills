class Food

attributes:
1. type (array)
2. temp (float)
3. price (float)
4. rating (float)

methods:
1. add_discount (changes the total price by inputing a discount to the food.)

2. change temp (change the current temp by adding or subtracting the temperature)

3. add_rating = adding rating to the original rating.

4. add_type = add new types of that certain food.
